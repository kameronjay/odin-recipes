Chicken Katsu Recipe

Behold the mighty chicken katsu!

This chicken katsu recipe is a delicious dish that is a favorite among Japanese households. Typically feeding four people, this recipe can be adjusted to feed as many people as desired by the chef...you!

Starting with four chicken breast halves, flour, egg, and panko breading, simply put all the ingredients together in order and fry to your heart's content. You now have the perfect chicken katsu dish to impress your friends!

Ingredients:

  • 4 chicken breast halves
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 cup panko bread crumbs
  • salt and pepper to taste
    •

    Steps:

    1. Season the chicken breasts on both sides with salt and pepper. Place the flour, egg and panko crumbs into separate shallow dishes. Coat the chicken breasts in flour, shaking off any excess. Dip them into the egg, and then press into the panko crumbs until well coated on both sides.
    2. Heat 1/4 inch of oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Place chicken in the hot oil, and cook 3 or 4 minutes per side, or until golden brown.
